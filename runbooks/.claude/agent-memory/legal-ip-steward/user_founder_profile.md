Dennis Lumumba Mukhavani (dennislumush@gmail.com) is the founder of Athlytica, Big Ice, and
NRHL — a sports data infrastructure venture based in Kenya, spanning African football
academy/scouting data and regional hockey operations. See [[project_venture_context]].

He operates through a structured multi-agent venture-orchestration pipeline (schema/product
design -> legal/IP review -> content strategy -> conversion systems), suggesting a systems-
oriented, process-driven approach to building out the venture rather than ad hoc execution.

**How to apply:** deliverables should be handed off in a form usable by non-legal downstream
collaborators (content strategists, conversion-system builders) without requiring them to
interpret dense legal text — favor explicit "can say / cannot say" claim guardrails over
open-ended legal risk discussion. Given the venture's core data categories (minors' biometric
data, cross-border athlete data), expect Kenya DPA 2019 and cross-border transfer questions to
recur across future sessions — keep [[reference_kenya_dpa_framework]] current.
