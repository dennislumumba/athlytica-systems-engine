Athlytica is a sports data infrastructure venture eliminating data voids in athlete scouting, tracking, and risk/insurance in emerging markets (African football academies, player agents, federations, insurers, plus NRHL/Big Ice regional hockey as a white-space strategic play). Founder: Dennis Lumumba Mukhavani (economics/statistics/elite athletics domain expertise). See [[user-dennis-founder]].

## Key model outputs (2026-07-08 snapshot)

**B2B tiers:** Schema (free, institutional-email-gated funnel top) → Academy/Club SaaS ($2K–$8K/yr flat or $50–$150/athlete/yr, priced ~90-95% below Catapult per-athlete cost since Athlytica has no hardware capex) → Agent/Agency ($200–$500/athlete profile/yr, flagged as structurally weak unit economics — see below) → Federation/Enterprise ($25K–$75K/yr, anchored to FIFA Forward Programme disbursement scale) → Insurer Data License ($40K–$150K/yr base + 8–15% rev-share on *new* premium volume unlocked) → NRHL/Big Ice ($1K–$5K/season, white-space/strategic not revenue-driven).

**Unit economics (CAC/LTV/payback):** All tiers pay back in 4.5–6 months EXCEPT the Agent/Agency tier, which has a ~21-month payback under a flat per-profile SaaS model — CAC ($1K-3K, relationship-driven) is too high relative to ARPU ($1,750/yr avg). Recommended fix: reposition agent tier toward a success-fee model (1-2% of documented transfer valuation uplift) rather than flat SaaS. This is a known weak point Agent 3 (offer architect) should design around, not smooth over.

**Success-fee structures:**
- Solidarity payment recovery (academy tier): 20% of recovered FIFA solidarity contribution (5% of transfer fee, distributed per Article 21/Annex 5 formula — 0.25%/yr registration ages 12-15, 0.5%/yr ages 16-23), contingency-based ($0 unless recovery succeeds). Worked modal case: $500K transfer → $6,250 recoverable → $1,250 to Athlytica, $5,000 to academy.
- Insurer new-premium rev-share: 8-15% of incremental new premium written using Athlytica risk data (never against the insurer's existing book — this is the "incremental insurability" framing).

**Funnel conversion assumptions:** 5-8% institutional download→qualified inquiry, 25-40% inquiry→paid consult, 30-50% consult→enterprise enrollment. ~0.7% overall download-to-customer, benchmarked against enterprise-infra/gated-technical-content B2B funnels (not consumer/ad funnels).

**Staged revenue targets:** Year 1 $15K-$40K (consults + NRHL/academy pilots, lowest-CAC segments first), Year 2 $150K-$350K (academy base scales, first solidarity-recovery success fees, federation conversation opens), Year 3 $600K-$1.2M (first federation + insurer contracts land, given their 12-24mo sales cycles). Framed explicitly as forward-looking projection, not fabricated actuals — no live customer revenue exists as of 2026-07-08.
