Source inputs as of 2026-07-08, feeding [[project_positioning_framework]].

**Named data voids / segments (Agent 1):**
- West/Central African football academies — CAF/FIFA U-17 bone-age (MRI) disputes; FIFA Clearing House Article 21 solidarity/training-compensation payment leakage (CIES/FIFPro-documented systemic underclaiming); agent-side info asymmetry suppressing transfer valuations.
- East African distance running (Kenya Rift Valley, Ethiopia) — no data layer linking district results to portable records; exploitative first-contact management contracts.
- Caribbean sprinting (Jamaica ISSA/Champs) — high-value NCAA/shoe-company scouting target, fragmented non-queryable results.
- NRHL/Big Ice inline/ice hockey — total white-space, zero standardized tracking infra, no incumbent to displace.
- Insurance/risk layer — catastrophic injury/career insurance for developmental athletes unpriceable, no actuarial baseline.

Buyer psychology is fear-driven, not aspiration-driven (academy directors fear sanctions/forfeited payments; agents fear being undercut by data-equipped European clubs; federations fear losing FIFA Forward Programme/Olympic Solidarity funding tranches tied to data-governance; insurers lack actuarial baseline). Buyers distrust Wyscout/InStat/Hudl/Catapult as structurally incompatible (video/wearable-first, built for funded pro/collegiate markets) and distrust foreign platforms extracting data without local institutional control — this is why the open-source schema is a genuine trust wedge, not just a lead-magnet gimmick. Competitive wedge: position as the verifiable capture/taxonomy layer BENEATH Wyscout/Catapult and FEEDING INTO FIFA Clearing House and insurer underwriting, not head-on competition.

**Pricing/mechanics (Agent 2):**
- Tier 0 Schema Access = $0, institutional email gate.
- Tier 1 Academy/Club SaaS = $2,000-$8,000/yr flat or $50-150/athlete/yr (~90-95% below Catapult hardware capex; below Wyscout/InStat $10K-40K).
- Tier 2 Agent/Agency = weak unit economics as flat $200-500/athlete/yr (~21mo CAC payback) — repositioned to a 1-2% valuation-uplift success fee stacked on top of the agent's own commission, or agency-level bundle.
- Tier 3 Federation/Enterprise = $25,000-75,000/yr, paid via grant disbursement (FIFA Forward/CAF/Olympic Solidarity), positioned as small line item against $250K-1M+/yr grant budgets.
- Tier 4 Insurer Data License = $40,000-150,000/yr base + 8-15% rev-share on NEW incremental premium volume only (never existing book).
- Tier 5 NRHL/Big Ice = $1,000-5,000/season, white-space/category-definition pricing, not a near-term revenue driver.
- Solidarity recovery success fee = 20% contingency on recovered FIFA Article 21 payments; modal anchor case is $500K transfer -> $6,250 recoverable -> $1,250 fee / $5,000 net to academy (NOT the $8M Osimhen-scale case, which is proof-point/marketing only, not the anchor).
- Funnel targets: 5-8% download->inquiry, 25-40% inquiry->consult, 30-50% consult->enrollment.
- Status: pre-revenue/early-stage — do not fabricate existing customers or traction in any copy.

**Why:** These are the only facts that make Athlytica copy non-generic; every positioning claim in [[project_positioning_framework]] traces to one of these named mechanisms.
