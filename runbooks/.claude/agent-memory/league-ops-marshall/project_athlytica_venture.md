Athlytica is a Kenya-based pre-revenue venture ("What KYC did for finance, Athlytica does for sport") run by founder Dennis Lumumba Mukhavani. Big Ice / NRHL (Nairobi Regional Hockey League) is the hockey vertical and is specifically the design-partner prospect for the "League" edition of the enterprise platform — the pricing tier must NOT be called "NRHL," it should be called "League."

Offer ladder: free OSS Scouting Passport Schema (email-gated) -> paid Data Void Audit diagnostic ($2,000-5,000) -> Athlytica Core enterprise platform (Academy/Agency/Federation/Insurer/League editions).

As of the 2026-07-08 synthesis (produced as Agent 10 of a 10-agent venture-orchestration pipeline), a full operational launch runbook was delivered covering: infra deployment sequencing (git init, schema files, public repo, LICENSE, landing page, ESP, video), Kenya DPA/legal compliance gates (ODPC registration, DPA templates, consent architecture), technical-critic fixes (overclaim language on the illustrative $500K/$6,250 example, NRHL->League rename, Agent-Agency pricing decision flat-fee vs valuation-uplift not yet made, undisclosed conflict of interest between Federation-tier subscriptions and academy-side success fees), and a first-90-days BD cadence recommending academies as the first buyer segment to approach (shortest sales cycle, clearest illustrative pain point) ahead of agencies/federations/insurers.

**Why this matters for future work:** any future league-ops-marshall work on this venture (tournament runbooks, evaluation pod distribution, NRHL scheduling) should assume the broader Athlytica funnel is still pre-deployment unless told otherwise, and should keep pricing-tier naming consistent (League, not NRHL) and treat the $500K/$6,250 figure as illustrative-only, never a real case, in any external-facing material.

**How to apply:** When asked to help with NRHL/Big Ice operational logistics, check whether it intersects with the Athlytica GTM funnel (e.g., League-edition design-partner conversations) and flag the compliance gates (Kenya DPA, ODPC) before recommending any real athlete data be collected or shared.
